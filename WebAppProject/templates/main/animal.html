Animals

Desert Biome

Camels are characterized by being one of the few animals in the world that can store water.

Snakes are characterized by their slithering movement, contracting and releasing their muscles.

Aquatic Biome

Stingrays are characterized by their sharp and lethal sting.

Dolphins are characterized by their playful mood and their astounding intelligence.

Ice Biome

Polar bears are characterized by their strength and protectiveness to their young ones.

Penguins are characterized by their ability to slide through ice.

Forest Biome

Deers are characterized by their long legs and powerful speed.

Falcons are characterized by their long and pointed wings which they can beat up very fast.
